### 3. 长难句式语法 (Complex Sentence Grammar)
#### 句式结构
- **句式类型**：复合句（主句+多个从句和分词短语）
- **结构分析**：主句 + 定语从句 + 现在分词短语作状语 + 介词短语作状语
- **语法要点**：定语从句、现在分词短语、介词短语的嵌套使用

#### 例句分析
- **原句**：As the world grapples with mounting waste management challenges, the transition to a circular economy has gained momentum, with businesses and governments implementing strategies that can minimize waste generation, maximize resource recovery through recycling and composting, and reduce the environmental impact of consumption patterns that have traditionally relied on linear models of production and disposal.
- **结构拆解**：
  - 主句：the transition to a circular economy has gained momentum
  - 时间状语从句：As the world grapples with mounting waste management challenges
  - with复合结构：with businesses and governments implementing strategies
  - 定语从句：that can minimize waste generation, maximize resource recovery through recycling and composting, and reduce the environmental impact of consumption patterns（修饰 strategies）
  - 定语从句：that have traditionally relied on linear models of production and disposal（修饰 patterns）
- **翻译**：随着世界努力应对日益严峻的废物管理挑战，向循环经济的转变已获得动力，企业和政府正在实施能够最小化废物产生、通过回收和堆肥最大化资源回收并减少传统上依赖生产和处置线性模式的消费模式的环境影响的策略。
- **语法解析**：这是一个复杂的复合句，主句使用现在完成时表示已经产生的影响。As引导的时间状语从句表示同时性，with复合结构提供补充信息，两个that引导的定语从句分别修饰strategies和patterns。这种结构使句子信息丰富且逻辑清晰。
