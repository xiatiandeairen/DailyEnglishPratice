### 3. 长难句式语法 (Complex Sentence Grammar)
#### 句式结构
- **句式类型**：复合句（主句+多个从句和分词短语）
- **结构分析**：主句 + 定语从句 + 现在分词短语作状语 + 介词短语作状语
- **语法要点**：定语从句、现在分词短语、介词短语的嵌套使用

#### 例句分析
- **原句**：As governments address social challenges and inequality, social welfare programs and policies are being developed to provide support for vulnerable populations, reduce poverty, and ensure access to essential services such as healthcare, education, and housing, though designing effective social policies requires balancing assistance with incentives for self-sufficiency and considering the fiscal sustainability of programs that can have long-term impacts on government budgets.
- **结构拆解**：
  - 主句：social welfare programs and policies are being developed
  - 时间状语从句：As governments address social challenges and inequality
  - 不定式短语：to provide support for vulnerable populations, reduce poverty, and ensure access to essential services（作目的状语）
  - such as引导的举例：such as healthcare, education, and housing
  - 让步状语从句：though designing effective social policies requires balancing assistance with incentives for self-sufficiency and considering the fiscal sustainability of programs
  - 定语从句：that can have long-term impacts on government budgets（修饰 programs）
- **翻译**：随着政府应对社会挑战和不平等，正在制定社会福利计划和政策，为弱势群体提供支持、减少贫困并确保获得医疗、教育和住房等基本服务，尽管设计有效的社会政策需要平衡援助与自给自足的激励，并考虑可能对政府预算产生长期影响的计划的财政可持续性。
- **语法解析**：这是一个复杂的复合句，主句使用现在进行时的被动语态表示持续的动作。As引导的时间状语从句表示同时性，不定式短语作目的状语，such as引导的举例，though引导的让步状语从句表示对比，that引导的定语从句修饰programs。这种结构使句子信息丰富且逻辑清晰。
